History

HISTORY

    2020

  •  

    Maxpower will continue to produce professional level qualified and innovated products and provide excellent global tools service.

  •  


    Maxpower became the chairman of China Hardware Tools & Hardware Associations; Achieved honor of “National Technological Innovation Model Enterprise” AAAA-level standardized good behavior enterprise, a member unit of the Technical Committee of Tools and Hardware Branch. Hosted and participated in the formulation and revision of 83 product standards.

    2018

  •  


    Maxpower achieved honor as "Individual Champion Model of Manufacturing Enterprise".

  •  


    Maxpower R&D Centre was awarded "China National Technology Centre" by the China central Government, which is an unique company award in the hardware and tools fields.

    Maxpower acquired a local ventilating fan and environmental protection equipment factory.
